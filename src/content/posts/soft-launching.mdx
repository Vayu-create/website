date: "25.01.25"

I recently learned that most people keep their ideas to themselves and expect a mega launch moment where 
their thing goes viral and they live happily ever after. You're more likely to be hit by an asteroid than to this to happen.
Instead you should become that annoying friend who's always soft launching dumb things. 



Fail with your friends first then go conquer the world
